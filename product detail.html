Product Description:

Sourced from the misty highlands of Colombia's Huila region, JavaJunkies Product One is a single-origin Arabica bean celebrated for its rich aroma and smooth complexity. Each batch is hand-picked from small farms at elevations above 1,600 metres, where the slow maturation process enriches the bean's natural sugars and oils.

Upon grinding, the coffee releases distinct notes of toasted almond, dark cocoa, and a hint of citrus peel. Its aroma is warm and inviting — nutty, slightly floral, with a caramel undertone that hints at the depth within.

Brewing characteristics:
- French press: Reveals fuller body and earthy tones
- Espresso: Intensifies the cocoa notes with creamy crema
- Pour over: Highlights the citrus brightness

On the palate, expect a medium-bodied brew with:
- Low acidity
- Velvety mouthfeel
- Long, balanced finish
- Lingering hints of molasses and red fruit

Ideal for morning rituals or slow afternoons. Pairs excellently with:
- Oat milk
- Dark chocolate
- Nutty pastries

Roasted weekly in small batches to preserve freshness and nuance, Product One is for those who seek more than caffeine — it's a brew rooted in place, people, and precision.
